# Brainstorming

This skill facilitates creative problem-solving and design exploration.

## Instructions

1. Understand the user's goal and constraints.
2. Generate multiple ideas or approaches to solve the problem.
3. Evaluate the pros and cons of each approach.
4. Select the best approach based on the project's requirements and best practices.
5. Refine the selected approach into a concrete plan or design.
